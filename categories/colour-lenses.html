Lens Care Guide

Proper care of your colored contact lenses is essential for maintaining eye health and extending the life of your lenses.

  • 1
    Always wash your hands

    Before handling your lenses, wash your hands thoroughly with soap and dry them with a lint-free towel.

  • 2
    Use proper solution

    Only use contact lens solution recommended by your eye care professional. Never use water or saliva.

  • 3
    Replace on schedule

    Follow the replacement schedule prescribed for your lenses, whether they're daily, biweekly, or monthly.
